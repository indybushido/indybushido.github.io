Classes

Training is based on Isshinryu Karate which is a traditional Okinawan karate style. Our classes are geared in such a way that almost anyone any age can participate if they have the desire. We offer continuing classes with 4 sessions a year: Fall, Winter (Short Session), Spring, and Summer. The location and days change depending on whether or not school is in session.

During the School Year

Location

Skiles Test Elementary School
7001 Johnson Rd,
Indianapolis, IN 46220

We enter through the back of school on the southside, through the gym doors. We are not connected with Skiles Test Elementary in anyway. We rent an area for classes.

Time

  • Tuesday and Thursday evenings from 6:30PM to 7:30PM
  • Open training for intermediate and advanced from 7:35PM until 8:00PM.

Snow days

If the school closes due to snow, the facility will be closed and we will not have classes. We always work with students to makeup those days.
