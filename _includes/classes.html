Classes

Training is based on Isshinryu Karate which is a traditional Okinawan karate style. Our classes are geared in such a way that almost anyone any age can participate if they have the desire. We offer continuing classes with 4 sessions a year: Fall, Winter (Short Session), Spring, and Summer. The location and days change depending on whether or not school is in session.
