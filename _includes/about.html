About

Indy Bushido has been teaching karate and self defense in Lawrence Township, on the Northeast side of Indianapolis, since 1997.

We offer family friendly classes, ages 7 - adult. Building character, confidence, discipline and sportsmanship in our students is our priority, while teaching the traditional martial art of Isshinryu Karate.

  • 1997

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Indianapolis Isshinryu Bushido Club was formed by long-time students of Master John C. Lennox, upon his retirement in 1997.

  • 1997-2009

    Lawrence Community Education

    The school operated as a program within Lawrence Community Education for 12 years. At which time all proceeds were given to Lawrence Community Education.

  • 2009

    Transition to an LLC

    The new owner Sensei John Hoffmire, 8th Dan, was a student of Sensei John C. Lennox. The other LLC owners (Sensei Larry Tylenda, 5th Dan and Sensei Shellee Tylenda, 4th Dan), as well as all our other Sensei, are students of Sensei Hoffmire and have trained at Indy Bushido for many years.
